# Topic-modelling-and-text-classification
The research highlights the importance of Business Intelligence and Artificial Intelligence in making better business decisions, with a predicted shortage of professionals with deep analytics skills. It describes big data analytics as a rapidly emerging technology with the potential to transform business operations, combining advanced techniques such as business intelligence and decision support systems to analyse extensive data. The paper also covers the field of knowledge engineering, its relation to business intelligence, and the practical application of BI in the business world, including standard tools such as data mining and natural language processing. The research concludes by discussing the potential of integrating knowledge engineering and BI to unlock new value from data and enable more sophisticated decision-making, providing a unique perspective for decision support systems in the marketing department
